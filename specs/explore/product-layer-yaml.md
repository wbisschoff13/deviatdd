# Exploration: Product-Layer YAML Handover Emission

## Problem Definition

[Statement]: Extend the existing Content Capture (FLOW-11) YAML-handover pattern — currently applied to the 15 macro/meso/micro phase skills listed in `tests/test_handover/test_skill_prompts.py::PHASE_SKILLS` — to the four Product-layer skills (`deviate-constitution`, `deviate-flows`, `deviate-architecture`, `deviate-release`) so that `deviate content` (FLOW-12) can aggregate handover YAMLs across **all** phases/layers, not just the 15 listed.

[Scope]: In-scope structural components verified across the scan:
- The 15-skill handover emission contract at `tests/test_handover/test_skill_prompts.py` (one-sentence Write instruction appended to the terminal-contract block, referencing `handover_path()` as a deterministic idempotency marker).
- The path-convention decision at `src/deviate/core/handover.py::handover_path()` (canonical target `.deviate/feat/<epic>/<issue>/[<task>/]<phase>.yaml`).
- The four Product-layer skill directories at `src/deviate/prompts/skills/{deviate-constitution,deviate-flows,deviate-architecture,deviate-release}/SKILL.md`.
- The Content Capture architecture at `specs/_product/architecture.md` §3.5–§3.7 and §4.4, which governs the path convention for `.deviate/feat/`.
- The Constitution v0.3.0 Content Capture exception (`specs/constitution.md:14-18`).
- The narrative-anchor field map at `specs/plans/deviate-content.md:52-67` (12 phases; product-layer phases are absent).
- The synthesis layer at `src/deviate/core/synthesis.py` and the read-side `HandoverRecord` model at `src/deviate/core/handover.py::HandoverRecord`.
- The gitignore surface at `.deviate/.gitignore` (`/feat/`, `/content-drafts/` already excluded).
- The product-layer staging directory at `specs/_product/` (constitution, architecture, domain-model, release-next, flows/, flows/index.md).

[Exclusions]: Architectural decisions, design trade-offs, risk analysis, naming of any new product-layer anchor fields, decisions about whether to amend `handover_path()` to accept a `None` epic/issue, whether to introduce a new `_product/` subdirectory under `.deviate/feat/`, narrative_anchor field design for product-layer phases, synthesis-layer changes for product-layer outputs, and failure-mode speculation — all deferred to the `deviate-research` skill. This scan catalogs what exists; it does not propose how to wire product-layer handovers into FLOW-11.
